90-Day Roadmap

Ideal for: Seed-stage founders, companies rebuilding after a failed marketing hire, or pre-Series A teams that need clarity and a plan before spending more on marketing.

What it is

A structured, one-time engagement that gives you an honest, data-backed assessment of your entire marketing function — and a 90-day prioritised action plan your team can execute the following Monday. No slides that collect dust. Decisions from day one. I dig into your funnel data, ICP, positioning, channel performance, martech stack, and competitive positioning — and come back with a clear, ordered diagnosis and roadmap.

What's included

  • —Full-funnel audit: traffic sources, lead volume, conversion rates, CAC, pipeline coverage by channel
  • —ICP refinement workshop: who you're actually selling to vs. who you should be targeting — and what changes
  • —Positioning and messaging review: how you show up vs. your top 3 competitors — gap analysis and recommendations
  • —Channel assessment: which channels are working, which are wasting budget, and which are entirely missing
  • —Martech stack review: gaps, redundancies, attribution model evaluation, and recommended tooling changes
  • —90-day prioritised roadmap with OKRs, quick wins (weeks 1–4), and longer-range plays (months 2–3)
  • —2-hour readout session with CEO/founder: findings, decisions, and alignment on the plan
  • —30-day follow-up call: reviewing execution progress and unblocking any obstacles in the plan

About

12 years of building
B2B SaaS revenue engines.

From a freelance consultant running Google Ads on a shoestring in Pune, to VP of Marketing at a GenAI SaaS company — and now Fractional CMO to founders who need the same firepower.

CMO Alliance RevGenius ProductLed PMA SaaS Alliance

I run marketing as a revenue system — a function the CEO, CFO, and board can underwrite.

I started in 2014 running Google Ads for a security services company in Pune on a budget that would make most paid media managers laugh. No playbook. No agency. No team. Just a problem to solve, a number to hit, and a feedback loop. That operator mentality — obsessing over what actually moves the needle — has defined every role I've taken since.

Over 12 years across B2B SaaS, martech, salestech, and B2B media, I've led marketing at every stage of the journey: building from absolute zero, rebuilding functions that weren't working, and scaling engines that were already moving. My work spans the full stack of modern marketing leadership — category narrative, product marketing, demand generation, PLG and lifecycle, brand and analyst relations, martech and attribution, and team architecture.

At Clodura.AI, I joined as the first marketing hire and built the entire function — team, martech stack, GTM motion, and brand — from nothing. The result: 3.5× ARR growth, 40% CAC reduction, 2,500%+ organic traffic growth, a $500K AppSumo campaign, and a $2M+ pre-Series A fundraise I co-authored the GTM narrative for. At Machintel, I managed a 15-person global team across 30+ brands and built the Tableau attribution stack that became the reporting backbone for leadership reviews.

"Marketing is a revenue system. If it doesn't show up in pipeline, it isn't marketing — it's a cost centre waiting to be cut."

After a decade of building inside companies, I made a decision: the founders and CEOs who need CMO-level thinking most are exactly the ones who can't yet justify a full-time hire. I'm here to change that equation. As your Fractional CMO, I embed into your team — accountable to the same metrics your board cares about, without the full-time overhead.

I work with B2B SaaS companies across India, US, and EU — Seed to Series B — who have product-market fit, are ready to build a real marketing engine, and want a partner who's been in the CMO seat before.
